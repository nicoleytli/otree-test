In this experiment you will decide whether you prefer to play a lottery, in which the payoff is dependent on the color of a ball randomly drawn from a drum, or to receive a guaranteed amount of money.

Before it starts, you need to choose one of the envelopes. Each envelope contains a random number from 0.00 to 2.00. All possible numbers have equal probability. These numbers are randomly preselected by the computer and will remain the same all through the experiment.. Once experiment 2 is over, we will give you a password to open your envelope on the third party website: https://www.browserling.com/tools/aes-decrypt. Then you can get your result.
