This activity is part of a study in decision-making. You will be asked to do three experiments.
In each experiment, you need to choose one of the encrypted messages which contain your payment information, and answer one related question. The messages in different experiments are encrypted with different keys, algorithms and modes. After each experiment, we will give you the details to decrypt your message on a third-party website: https://codebeautify.org/encrypt-decrypt. Meanwhile, we will decrypt your message as well to prepare for your payment.
